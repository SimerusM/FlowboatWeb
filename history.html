What is this page?

The History section of our website is a record of the Flowboat Startup Incubator's past. It lists former members and administrators, along with the startups that came to life each year. The winner of our annual Flowboat Pitching Competition is also announced here, allowing you to track our progress and see the innovative companies we've helped shape over time.
